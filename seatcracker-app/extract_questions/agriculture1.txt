Sexual reproduction in plants was discovered by:
(1)Linnaeus
(2)Camerarius
(3)Hutchinson
(4) Bessey
Correct Answer: (2) Camerarius
4
Solution:
Rudolf Jacob Camerarius, a German botanist and physician, is credited with being the first to
scientifically demonstrate the process of sexual reproduction in flowering plants. His seminal
work, published in 1694, titled “De sexu plantarum epistola” (Letter on the sex of plants),
clearly outlined the role of male (pollen) and female (ovule) components in plant
reproduction. This work provided the foundation for modern botanical genetics and was a
monumental step forward in the study of plant biology.
